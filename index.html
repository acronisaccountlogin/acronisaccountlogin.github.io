Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in to my Acronis account?

Open the Acronis application or login page, enter your registered email address and password, then complete any required verification to access your dashboard.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Select the password recovery option on the login page, follow the verification instructions, and create a new secure password.

Can I manage multiple devices from one account?

Yes. Your Acronis account allows you to manage supported devices, backups, and subscriptions from one centralized dashboard.

What can I do after signing in?

You can manage backups, restore files, download software, update your subscription, review security settings, and monitor protected devices.
